Introduction

We have decided to create a supplemental website for National College of Ireland. The main goal of our website is to show future students what can be done in terms of web development after attending the Web Design and Client-Side Scripting module. To make our website fit seamlessly with the original NCI website: www.ncirl.ie we have adopted its style, layout, and general function.
